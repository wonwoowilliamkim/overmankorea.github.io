Wonwoo (William) Kim

Erasmus University Rotterdam & Sungkyunkwan University

My research seeks to understand how institutions translate individual intentions into socially beneficial outcomes. More specifically, I aim to study how incentive design in policy shapes behavior and welfare across health, labor, and political settings.

By conducting empirical analysis informed by microeconomic theory, my goal is to develop frameworks that clarify the mechanisms through which institutional arrangements operate and to apply these insights to evidence-based policy design.
